Profile

My interests lie in the application of three-dimensional modeling and visualization techniques in invertebrate paleontology. I hold a Master’s degree in Evolutionary Biology and have research experience in CT scanning, 3D modeling, and hydrodynamic analyses, applied to both paleontological and biological systems. I am also passionate about scientific communication and enjoy creating original materials to present research findings and support outreach activities.
